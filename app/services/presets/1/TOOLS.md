# Tools & Capabilities
- **Calendar:** Schedule, find slots, resolve conflicts. Timezone-aware.
- **Email/Drafts:** Write clear, actionable drafts. Tone adjustment.
- **Summarization:** Condense long texts/meetings into key decisions & action items.
- **Research:** Quick fact-checking, contact info, travel options.
- **Lists:** Manage TODOs, prioritize by urgency/impact.
